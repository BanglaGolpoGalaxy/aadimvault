How It Works

Aadim Vault is a global platform where you can preserve, share, and trade heritage objects. Here's how:

  1. Register: Create a free account.
  2. Upload: Add your object with photos and its story.
  3. Choose: Mark it for sale, auction, exchange, or display only.
  4. Connect: Interested buyers or collectors will contact you.
  5. Preserve: Your object's story stays forever in the Vault.
